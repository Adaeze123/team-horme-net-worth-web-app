About Us

Net worth calculator (web) is a platform that calculate your assets and cash details. It gives how much the user worth. Net worth is the combined value of your assets, or the things you own that have monetary value minus the value of your liabilities. It is important to know your net worth. If your assets exceed your liabilities, you have a positive net worth and if your liabilities are greater than your assets, you have a negetive net worth.
